About


We are a team of five students from Łódź University of Technology interested in Game Development. We started working together in 2017 and are currently focused on two projects. Our main game is called "IronHenge" and it is a different take on Tower Defence targeted towards mobile platforms developed using Unity Engine. Second project - "Desolate: Pray for Silence" is quite different, it is a horror game taking place on spaceships invaded by mutants in the future, developed using our own engine based on Monogame framework.

IronHenge
This mobile game shows a new way to look at Tower Defence genre. The world suffers from a disaster caused by excessive sun activity. The only way of survival is going under ground to find water. Hostile life forms forced humans to find a way to defend themselves. Rotating rings were built around the caves to protect their residents. Give people hope in this sun torched hell, play the role of defence system administrator and spin it in your favour!


Desolate: Pray for Silence Stelth, horror, interplanet space travels and enormous, mutated cockroaches. Even an expert in their extermination can't always manage. Try taking his place! You are being woken up from sleeping in a hibernation chamber during tourist cruise to Mars. You have to do everything in your power to save other travellers. But be careful, they musn't hear you... Step into the shoes of cosmic insect's exterminator and check if you manage. Get rid of them from the ship, complete an assigment and don't let them get you!
